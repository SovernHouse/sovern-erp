The Mandarin and Taiwanese fluency is a material business advantage. ### Revenue Model

**Primary: Buying commission — 5% of FOB factory price (fixed and transparent)**
The buyer sees the factory price; New Route earns 5% on top. No hidden spread. This positions New Route unambiguously as a buying house working *for* the client. Industry standard for Asia sourcing agents is 5–10%; 5% is competitive and trust-building.

**Variable rates apply for:**
- Smaller orders (higher % to cover fixed sourcing overhead)
- Private label and brand development (design, sampling, certification, packaging)
- QC inspection as a standalone service
- Complex multi-factory or multi-category sourcing programs

**Secondary: Principal trading (buy-sell spread)**
Used selectively where New Route has strong sourcing advantage and the buyer prefers a single delivered price. Margins typically 8–15%. Should be structured transparently given the buying house positioning — never mix models without clarity.

**Future: ERP licensing/SaaS**
The Trading ERP can be licensed to other trading companies. New Route's live deployment is the proof-of-concept and demo environment.
