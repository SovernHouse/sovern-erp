# Sovern House — Cold Email Writer Skill

**Version:** 1.0 | **Last Updated:** April 2026  
**Depends on:** sovern-icp.md (read first), company-profile.md  
**Use for:** Writing first-touch cold emails to qualified prospects.

---

## The Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **50–125 words per email.** Research-backed sweet spot for reply rates. If it's longer, cut it.
2. **One ask per email.** Not "let me know if you're interested AND here's our brochure AND can we schedule a call." One clear next step.
3. **No attachments on cold emails.** They trigger spam filters and create friction. Links to landing pages only.
4. **Personalize the first line.** The opener must prove you looked at them specifically. No "I came across your company" generic openers.
5. **Compliance:** Include Sovern House physical address (Taiwan) in footer. Make unsubscribe easy. Never claim guaranteed outcomes.

---

## Sovern House Brand Voice for Email

- **Direct, not pushy.** Say what you do and what it's worth to them. No filler.
- **Human, not corporate.** Alex has 30 years on the ground. That's a person, not a platform.
- **Evidence > claims.** "We negotiate in Mandarin directly with factory owners" beats "We have deep supplier relationships."
- **No Western trade clichés.** Skip: "synergies," "best-in-class," "seamless," "one-stop shop." Use plain language.
- **Respect cultural neutrality.** Buyers in EU, US, and LatAm read these. No idioms, sports metaphors, or culture-specific humor.

## Email Architecture (use this structure every time)

```
SUBJECT LINE
─────────────────────────────
LINE 1:  Specific observation about THEM (1 sentence, personalized)
LINE 2:  What we do + how it's relevant to their situation (1-2 sentences)  
LINE 3:  Proof / credibility hook (1 sentence — numbers, specifics, differentiator)
LINE 4:  Single CTA (1 sentence — low friction ask)
─────────────────────────────
SIGNATURE + footer
```

---

## Subject Line Formulas

Subject lines should be 4–7 words, lowercase preferred (feels less like marketing).

**Problem-aware:**
- `sourcing from [country] — quick question`
- `your [flooring/garments/etc.] suppliers in Asia`
- `Asia QC — are you covered?`
- `[Company name] + Asia sourcing`

**Curiosity / relevance:**
- `re: your [product category] line`
- `something for your Asia sourcing`
- `[their city] importer + Taiwan sourcing`

**Direct:**
- `buying office in Asia — [Company name]`
- `5% flat fee, on-the-ground QC`

Avoid: exclamation marks, ALL CAPS, emojis, "FREE", "opportunity", question marks in every subject.

## Follow-Up Email Templates

### Follow-up 1 (Day 4–5): Add value, don't just bump

**Subject:** `re: [original subject]`

> Hi [First name],
>
> Following up from last week. Thought this might be useful context:
>
> [One concrete, relevant fact — e.g., "CARB Phase 2 enforcement has tightened this year — importers without factory-level documentation are getting flagged at the border more often." OR "Taiwan sourcing lead times are running 6–8 weeks right now — planning ahead matters."]
>
> Happy to talk through how this affects your category specifically.
>
> [Signature]

---

### Follow-up 2 (Day 9–10): Reframe the ask

**Subject:** `different question for you`

> Hi [First name],
>
> I'll keep this brief — are you the right person to speak with about Asia sourcing at [Company], or would someone else be a better fit?
>
> Happy to reach out to the right contact if you can point me in the right direction.
>
> [Signature]

---

### Follow-up 3 (Day 14–15): The break-up

**Subject:** `closing the loop`

> Hi [First name],
>
> I've reached out a couple of times — I'll take the silence as a "not right now," which is completely fine.
>
> If Asia sourcing or QC ever becomes a priority for [Company], feel free to reach out. We'll be here.
>
> Best of luck with the business.
>
> [Signature]

## Pre-Send Checklist

Before sending any cold email, verify:

- [ ] Prospect matches ICP (sovern-icp.md) — if not, do not send
- [ ] First line is personalized to THIS specific company or contact
- [ ] Email is 50–125 words
- [ ] Single CTA only
- [ ] No attachments
- [ ] Subject line is under 7 words, no spam triggers
- [ ] Signature includes physical address
- [ ] Compliance screened (OFAC name check for new contacts)
- [ ] Send time: Monday–Thursday, 9:30–11:30am recipient's local timezone (not Friday)
